If You Are Not A Current Client Of Our Firm:

It is always possible that we already represent someone else who is adverse to you concerning the subject matter of your communication.

  1. You have had direct communication in person or by telephone with one of our attorneys;
  2. KKG has cleared possible conflicts of interest;
  3. You and KKG have agreed to the terms of our engagement; and
  4. You and KKG have confirmed the terms in writing with an engagement letter.
