YOUR SOLUTION FOR CUSTOM

PACKAGING

We are an American packaging company that has been actively providing high-quality packaging solutions to global beauty and skincare brands since 2006.

With our headquarters in Florida and our own manufacturing facility in China, we are in the unique position to offer our clients the best of both worlds.

By operating locally, we are better able to communicate with our clients and understand their markets, while having an overseas manufacturing facility under our full control allows us to offer better quality and better production times at better prices.

OUR FACILITIES

TOTAL CONTROL OVER QUALITY

One of the biggest benefits of owning and operating our own manufacturing facility is that we have total control over the quality and consistency of the products we create and sell, and we are extremely proud of our facility.

With over 75,000 square ft of manufacturing space, our facility is one of the largest acrylic container producers in China.

GREEN INITIATIVES

We have also implemented green initiatives focused on eliminating waste and promoting efficiency, such as decreasing and redeploying any resin material waste and adding equipment and standards that exceed governmental requirements regarding waste water and gas treatment for our molding, metal, and decorating facilities.

GREEN INITIATIVES

To add another layer of security to ensure that you will receive consistency, excellence, and value every time you work with us, we have gone to the extra step to obtain ISO certification for our factory.

Currently we are both ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 certified. We are also certified with, and audited by, some of the major international brands with whom we work.

We always put our customers first

OUR PROMISE

We prioritize excellent customer service and prompt communication, and we are always open and honest about what can and cannot be done – and we will never over-promise something we cannot deliver.

We always strive to think for our customers, even if it means suggesting less-costly packaging options, and we ensure that we are always acting in the best interested of our clients.

OUR PRODUCTS

We offer both stock and custom packaging products, depending on our clients’ needs. Our stock products are carefully curated and kept on-hand in our Ft. Lauderdale warehouse, ready to ship at a moment’s notice, while our custom products can be made, finished, and printed to meet the unique look of your brand, through an array of customization services.

Whether you are looking for high quality stock items or the best in customized packaging, we have the best products and services to meet your needs and exceed your expectations.
